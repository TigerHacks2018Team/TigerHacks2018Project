About the Newsroom

The Newsroom is a site for the collaboration of journalists and reporters. A multimedia directory database for all photos, interview recordings, timelines. A networking hub where multiple writers and reporters can work together on stories at one time.

Features

  • A multimedia database for photos, video, interview recordings and more.
  • A hub for journalists to collaborate on stories and work together.
